Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

At All Age Care, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth
  • Medical Information: Health conditions, medications, care requirements
  • Payment Information: Billing details and payment method information
  • Emergency Contact Information: Names and contact details of emergency contacts

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide the best possible care services:

  • Match you with appropriate licensed caregivers
  • Develop personalized care plans
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Communicate important updates and schedule changes
  • Improve our services based on feedback and needs

Information Sharing

We value your trust and never sell your personal information. We only share information:

  • With your consent
  • With caregivers who need it to provide services
  • When required by law
  • To protect rights and safety

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
