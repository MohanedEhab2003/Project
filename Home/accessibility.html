Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment to Accessibility

All Age Care is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Full functionality available using keyboard only
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Works with major screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Adjustable Text Sizes: Text can be resized using browser controls
  • High Contrast Mode: Sufficient color contrast for readability
  • Alternative Text: All images include descriptive alt text
  • Clear Heading Structure: Properly nested headings for easy navigation

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. Our ongoing accessibility efforts include regular testing and updates to maintain compliance.

Assistive Technologies

Our website is compatible with:

  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Text-to-speech browsers
  • Alternative input devices

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content may not be fully accessible. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us and we will provide alternative access or fix the issue promptly.

Accessible Care Services

Beyond our website, we ensure our physical care services are accessible:

  • Caregivers trained in supporting individuals with diverse needs
  • Alternative communication methods available (sign language, written, etc.)
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate specific requirements
  • Assistance with mobility and daily tasks

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We conduct regular accessibility audits, provide training to our team, and update our practices as standards evolve.
